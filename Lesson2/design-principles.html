www.apple.com

This page effectively presents the pillars of a White Space and Clean Design, where it can be appreciated that it uses white space to create a clean and organized design and highlight the most important elements. Its typography is legible and coherent to facilitate reading and appearance, with consistent colors and simplicity in navigation. The page also features high-quality images and graphics that are consistent with the website's aesthetic. PARC: Contrast

Figma

www.figma.com

Figma utilizes contrast to highlight its products and services, differentiating between tones, colors, or sizes of two or more different elements. It creates contrast through the use of different sizes, fonts, typographies, shapes, and white spaces to create a clear and effective visual hierarchy.
